Frequently asked questions

What does a Google Business Profile management service do?

It handles the ongoing work of optimizing and maintaining your profile — categories, services, photos, posts, Q&A, review responses, and the local signals that drive 3-pack visibility. See our GBP service.

How much does GBP management cost?

Most managed GBP services run $100–$500 per month per location, while DIY listing tools start around $14/month. Full-service local SEO that includes GBP work typically runs $300–$2,000/month. See our local SEO pricing guide.

Can I manage my Google Business Profile myself?

Yes — claiming the profile, adding photos, and gathering reviews can all be done in-house. Most businesses bring in help for competitive optimization, citation consistency, suspension risk, and ongoing posting.

What happens if my profile gets suspended?

A suspension removes you from Maps entirely. A good management service handles reinstatement and reduces the risk by keeping your profile within Google’s guidelines. RankLocAll includes suspension recovery.
