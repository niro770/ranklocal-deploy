What local SEO actually includes

Not all local SEO companies offer the same scope of work. Here are the core services to expect from a full-service local SEO partner:

Google Business Profile optimization

Complete setup, category selection, service areas, photo optimization, Q&A management, and posting schedule. This is the single highest-impact local SEO activity.

Citation building

Getting your business listed (with consistent NAP data) across Yelp, Yellow Pages, Angi, BBB, and 50+ other directories. Inconsistent data across directories hurts rankings.

On-page SEO

Optimizing title tags, meta descriptions, headers, and page content for local keywords. Every service area should have a dedicated, optimized page on your site.

Review management

Generating new Google reviews, responding to reviews, and managing your overall rating. Reviews are a direct ranking factor in the Google Maps algorithm.

Local link building

Earning links from local chambers of commerce, news sites, and community organizations. Local backlinks are one of the strongest signals for Maps rankings in competitive markets.

Local content creation

Writing city-specific service pages, blog posts answering local questions, and location landing pages — especially valuable for businesses serving multiple zip codes or cities.

Frequently asked questions

What does a local SEO company do?

A local SEO company helps businesses rank higher in local search results — particularly Google Maps and the local pack. Services typically include Google Business Profile optimization, local citation building, on-page SEO, review management, and local link building. According to Google, 76% of people who search for something nearby visit a business within a day, making local visibility critical for service businesses.

How much does local SEO cost?

Local SEO services typically cost between $300 and $2,000 per month depending on scope, number of locations, and market competitiveness. One-time citation building packages range from $200 to $800. Full-service retainers for competitive markets can exceed $3,000 per month. DIY tools like Moz Local start at $14/month per location.

How long does local SEO take to work?

Most local SEO campaigns show measurable improvement within 3 to 6 months. Google Business Profile optimization can produce results faster — sometimes within 4 to 8 weeks — for businesses in less competitive markets. Citation building and link building produce cumulative results that compound over 6 to 12 months.

What is the difference between local SEO and regular SEO?

Regular SEO targets broad national or global keyword rankings. Local SEO specifically targets location-based searches — queries like "HVAC company near me" or "best dentist in [city]" — and optimizes for Google Maps and the local pack. Local SEO focuses on Google Business Profile, NAP consistency, local citations, and geo-targeted content.

Can I do local SEO myself?

Basic local SEO can be done yourself — claim your Google Business Profile, add photos, gather reviews, and list your business in major directories. However, competitive markets require advanced work: technical on-page optimization, citation cleanup, local link building, and ongoing content creation. Most local businesses save time and get better results by using a professional local SEO company.
