What local SEO actually includes

Not all companies offer the same scope of work. Here are the core services a full-service local SEO partner should cover:

  • Google Business Profile optimization — Complete setup, category selection, service areas, photos, Q&A, and posting. This is the single highest-impact local SEO activity.
  • Citation building — Listing your business with consistent NAP data across Yelp, Yellow Pages, Angi, BBB, and 50+ directories. Inconsistent data across directories hurts rankings.
  • On-page SEO — Optimizing title tags, meta descriptions, headers, and content for local keywords. Every service area should have a dedicated, optimized page.
  • Review management — Generating new Google reviews, responding to existing reviews. Reviews are a direct ranking factor in the Maps algorithm.
  • Local link building — Earning links from local chambers, news sites, and community organizations. Local backlinks are one of the strongest signals in competitive markets.
  • Local content creation — City-specific service pages, blog posts answering local questions, and location landing pages for multi-city businesses.

Frequently asked questions

What does a local SEO company do?

A local SEO company helps businesses rank higher in local search results — particularly Google Maps and the local pack. Services typically include Google Business Profile optimization, citation building, on-page SEO, review management, and local link building. According to Google, 76% of people who search for something nearby visit a business within a day.

How much does local SEO cost?

Local SEO typically costs $300–$2,000/month depending on scope and market competitiveness. One-time citation building runs $200–$800. Full-service retainers in competitive markets can exceed $3,000/month. DIY tools like Moz Local start at $14/month per location.

How long does local SEO take to work?

Most local SEO campaigns show measurable improvement within 3–6 months. Google Business Profile optimization can produce results faster — sometimes within 4–8 weeks — for businesses in less competitive markets. Citation building and link building produce cumulative results that compound over 6–12 months.

What is the difference between local SEO and regular SEO?

Regular SEO targets broad national or global keyword rankings. Local SEO specifically targets location-based searches — queries like "HVAC company near me" or "best dentist in [city]" — and optimizes for Google Maps and the local pack through GBP optimization, NAP consistency, local citations, and geo-targeted content.

Can I do local SEO myself?

Basic local SEO can be done yourself — claim your Google Business Profile, add photos, gather reviews, and list your business in major directories. However, competitive markets require advanced work: technical optimization, citation cleanup, local link building, and ongoing content creation. Most local businesses get better results faster by working with a professional local SEO company.
