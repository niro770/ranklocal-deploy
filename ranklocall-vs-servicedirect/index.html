RankLocAll vs Service Direct: Which Pay-Per-Call Network Delivers Better Results?

Last updated: May 2026 • 8 min read

If you're a local service business shopping for pay-per-call leads, you've likely come across both RankLocAll and Service Direct. This side-by-side comparison covers pricing, verticals, call quality standards, and which platform tends to work better for different business types — so you can make an informed decision.

Quick verdict

RankLocAll is the stronger choice for local service businesses that want inbound calls only, strict call quality filtering, and no monthly minimums. Service Direct offers a broader mix of call and lead products, which suits businesses that want more volume flexibility but are comfortable with a wider variety of contact types.

Which one should you choose?

The right choice depends on what type of lead contact you want and whether you also need local SEO alongside your paid call campaigns.

Choose RankLocAll if you:

  • Want inbound phone calls only
  • Need strict call quality filtering
  • Don't want a monthly minimum or contract
  • Also need local SEO or Google Maps ranking
  • Operate in multiple verticals
  • Want dedicated account management

Consider Service Direct if you:

  • Want a mix of calls and form leads
  • Are in a mainstream home service vertical
  • Are comfortable with longer-term contracts
  • Don't need local SEO services

Which pay-per-call network is better for home services?
