Exclusive Contractor Leads: The Complete Explanation

By Nir Barlev, Founder & CEO · Updated July 2026

An exclusive contractor lead is delivered only to your business -- no other contractor receives the same contact. When a homeowner calls through a pay-per-call system, that call routes to your phone and only your phone. No competing contractors are simultaneously calling the same homeowner.

Exclusivity drives close rates because the homeowner conversation dynamic is completely different. With a shared lead, the homeowner knows multiple contractors are calling -- they're in 'selection mode' from the first contact. With an exclusive inbound call, the homeowner called you specifically. The conversation starts from a position where they're asking for your help, not evaluating you against three others they'll hear from in the next 20 minutes.

To verify exclusivity with any lead provider: ask specifically whether the same homeowner contact is sold to other contractors. Ask how many contractors can operate in the same service area (geographic exclusivity prevents neighboring contractors from bidding against you for the same traffic). Ask what happens if you're unavailable -- is the call forwarded to a competitor?

Frequently Asked Questions

What makes a contractor lead exclusive?

An exclusive lead is delivered only to your business -- not shared with competing contractors. With pay-per-call, exclusivity means an inbound call routes only to your phone. The homeowner doesn't receive simultaneous calls from competitors.

How do I verify that a lead is actually exclusive?

Ask the provider: Is this lead sold to other contractors? How many contractors operate in my service area? What happens if I miss a call -- is it forwarded to a competitor? Providers who can't answer these questions clearly may be selling 'semi-exclusive' leads.

Why do exclusive leads close at higher rates?

Exclusive leads start from a different homeowner dynamic: the homeowner called you, not a shared platform. They're not simultaneously evaluating multiple contractors. This single factor drives the 2-3x close rate advantage of exclusive vs shared leads.
