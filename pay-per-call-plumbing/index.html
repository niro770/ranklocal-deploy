Pay-Per-Call for Plumbing Contractors

By Nir Barlev, Founder & CEO · Updated July 2026

Plumbing is well-suited to pay-per-call because plumbing calls divide into two clear categories: emergency (burst pipe, sewage backup, failed water heater) and project (remodel, fixture upgrade, new construction support). Both convert well from inbound calls because the homeowner is actively searching and motivated to hire.

Emergency plumbing calls are among the most immediate-intent leads in home services. A homeowner with an active water emergency is calling every plumber they can find. Being the first to answer and confirm same-day arrival wins a very high percentage of these. Response speed is the entire close mechanism.
