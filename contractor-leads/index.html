FAQ

Common questions about contractor leads

What are contractor leads?
Contractor leads are inbound contacts from homeowners who need a specific home service — roofing, fencing, landscaping, pest control, or garage door repair. The quality and cost vary significantly by model. Shared leads go to multiple contractors; exclusive leads go only to you.
How much do home service leads cost?
Shared leads typically run $15–$60. Exclusive pay-per-call leads run $25–$75 per live inbound call. Booked appointments (pay-per-appointment) run $50–$200 per confirmed, qualified slot — the higher price reflects the higher close rate and zero qualifying work left for you.
What is the best lead generation for contractors?
Pay-per-appointment is the highest-quality model for most contractors with capacity to fill — you only pay for confirmed, exclusive, booked jobs. Learn how appointment setting works →
Are your contractor leads exclusive?
Yes. Every lead, call, and appointment we deliver goes exclusively to one contractor in your service area. We don't sell the same homeowner to three companies and call each one a lead.
Which trades do you cover?
Roofing, fencing, landscaping, pest control, and garage door across the US. If your trade isn't listed, reach out — we add verticals regularly.
