Best Lead Generation Options for Small Contractors

By Nir Barlev, Founder & CEO · Updated July 2026

Small contractors and solo operators don't need a large marketing budget to generate quality leads -- they need to focus on high-ROI, low-overhead options. Three channels consistently produce the best results for small contractors starting out.

1. Google Business Profile (free): Creating and optimizing a GBP is the most important first step for any contractor. A complete profile with photos, accurate categories, and consistent NAP generates free organic calls. Getting to 20+ Google reviews puts you in a competitive position in most local markets. This is zero variable cost after setup.

2. Google LSA: LSA has no monthly minimum and you pay only per lead. For a small contractor, $500-800/month in LSA can generate 8-15 qualified contacts depending on trade and market. The Google Guaranteed badge also builds trust that accelerates conversion.

3. Pay-per-call with no minimum: RankLocal's pay-per-call has no monthly minimum. You pay only for qualified calls that come in. For a small contractor testing a new lead channel, this is the lowest-risk entry point -- no wasted spend if call volume is lower than expected in the first month.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best lead generation option for a small contractor with a limited budget?

Start with Google Business Profile optimization (free), then add Google LSA ($500-800/month minimum). Add pay-per-call with no monthly minimum to test a third channel without overcommitting budget.

How much should a small contractor spend on lead generation?

3-8% of revenue is a typical range. A contractor doing $300K/year should budget $9,000-24,000 annually on marketing, or $750-2,000/month.

Can a solo contractor generate enough leads without a large marketing budget?

Yes. Google Business Profile plus consistent review generation can generate meaningful organic lead volume over 12-18 months at near-zero variable cost. Pair with Google LSA and pay-per-call for immediate paid lead flow.
