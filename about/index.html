How RankLocal started

In 2019, I was doing local SEO work for a handful of contractors and tradespeople in my city. What I kept noticing was frustrating: the best plumbers, the most experienced roofers, the most careful electricians — they were getting beaten in Google Maps by competitors who were clearly inferior, just because those competitors had better-optimized profiles.

"Great tradespeople were losing to worse competitors simply because of how their Google profiles were set up. The work wasn't the issue. The visibility was."

— RankLocal Founder, on why the agency was built

The gap wasn't in the quality of the work. It was in the visibility. And visibility in local search isn't magic — it's a set of concrete, learnable factors: the right categories, the right service structure, the right review cadence, the right citation footprint. We built RankLocal to systematize exactly that.

Six years later we've ranked over 100 businesses across the US — plumbers, roofers, HVAC contractors, landscapers, cleaning services, dentists, law firms. The industries change; the core problem doesn't. Every local business deserves to be found by the people who need them.

What we actually do differently

Most "local SEO" agencies touch your profile once and call it done. We work differently on three dimensions:

🔍
Full audit first, always

We find every fixable issue before we touch anything. Most local businesses have 8–14 issues we can address immediately. We don't start optimizing until we've mapped the complete gap.

📐
Category and attribute precision

The primary category on your Google profile is the single highest-weighted factor in Maps ranking. We pick the exact right one — a call most agencies get wrong because they default to the most obvious option, not the highest-ranking one.

⭐
Review strategy, not review requests

Sending "please leave us a review" texts doesn't scale. We build a repeatable review system for each client — timing, messaging, follow-up cadence — that averages 12–20 new reviews per month without feeling spammy.

📊
Month-by-month accountability

Month 1 we set the baseline. Month 2 we optimize what moved. Month 3 most clients are in the top 3. We report on what actually changed — ranking position, profile views, direction requests, call clicks — not vanity metrics.

Who we work with

We work with service-area businesses across the United States. Most of our clients are established contractors and service providers who are already good at their work — they just aren't showing up when their ideal customers search on Google. We've worked with plumbing companies, HVAC services, roofing contractors, landscaping businesses, house cleaning services, pest control companies, dental practices, and law firms.

We're not a fit for every business. If you're brand new, have no reviews, and haven't served any customers yet — there's a minimum foundation we need to build before local SEO can take hold. For businesses that have been operating for at least a year and have some customer history, the results are typically significant and fast.
