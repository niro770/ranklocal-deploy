How it works

Most lead generation leaves all the hard work to you. We handle the full path — from generating demand to confirming the booking.

FAQ

Common questions

What is appointment setting for contractors?
A service where we generate demand, qualify prospects against your criteria, and book confirmed jobs onto your calendar. You pay per appointment — not per click, impression, or raw lead.
How much does it cost?
Typically $50–$200 per qualified appointment depending on your trade and average job value. Roofing and remodeling sit near the top; pest control and lawn care sit lower. See full pricing breakdown →
What makes an appointment "qualified"?
Right service, inside your zip codes, homeowner or authorized decision-maker, real need with a real timeframe. Miss any of those four filters and it's credited — you don't pay. Full qualification standard →
Do I pay for no-shows?
No. We run reconfirmation before every appointment, and unqualified or no-show bookings are credited back. No provider should bill you for a missed appointment that cleared their own standard.
Can I control the volume?
Yes. You set the trades, zip codes, and weekly volume cap. Turn it up when you're slow, down when you're booked out. No minimums, no long-term lock-in.
Which trades do you cover?
Roofing, fencing, landscaping, pest control, garage door, and HVAC across the US. If your trade isn't listed, reach out — we add verticals regularly.
